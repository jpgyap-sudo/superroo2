As you may have heard, we're sunsetting the SuperRoo suite of tools on May 15th. This includes the SuperRoo VS Code Extension, SuperRoo Cloud, and SuperRoo Router.

To our community: thank you. Your trust, contributions, and feedback helped us drive innovation in this space and pushed us past 3m extension downloads. We don't take that lightly.

That said, we don't believe IDEs are the future of coding. To keep pushing the future forward, we needed to make this difficult decision. You can read more about the reasoning behind it [here](https://x.com/mattrubens/status/2046636598859559114).

## What this means for you

- We'll continue to support all existing SuperRoo products through May 15th.
- On May 15th, we'll shut down SuperRoo Cloud and SuperRoo Router, refund any unused balances, and archive the SuperRoo Extension repo.
- For billing questions after that, you can reach out to [billing@superroo.com](mailto:billing@superroo.com).

## Looking for an alternative?

If you want a model-agnostic open-source extension, we recommend [Cline](https://cline.bot/). They've incorporated much of what we built and are excited to welcome SuperRoo users.

If you're a fan of our cloud agents or curious about what we're building next, check out our new home at [roomote.dev](https://roomote.dev/).
